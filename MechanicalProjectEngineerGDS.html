Mechanical Project Engineer: GDS

Job description

We are looking for several passionate and motivated Senior Mechanical Project Engineers for Gas Distribution Systems (GDS) who will work with our Engineering and Construction teams to support and facilitate the GDS in our large-scale construction project. This candidate will have an interface with the Construction team to support planning and execution at different stages of the construction process. This candidate will also collaborate with the Engineering team to support the creation, modification, and implementation of gas delivery systems and assets. We are looking for individuals who prioritize safety, innovation, cost-effectiveness, and excellence.

This position will be based in Dresden, Germany

Duties:

  • Develop and implement project work plans and review as appropriate to meet changing needs and requirements.
  • Prepare engineering designs, specifications, studies, cost-estimates, and the development of sound technical recommendations.
  • Assist drafters in developing structural design of products using drafting tools, CAD, or other drafting equipment and software.
  • Research, design, evaluate, install, operate, and maintain mechanical products, equipment, systems and processes to meet dynamic requirements.
  • Supervise all construction types of work including: application drawing, piping, wall and floor opening, pipe rack, equipment foundation, equipment locating, welding argon management, management of electric panels for construction, punch management and improvement.
  • Perform troubleshooting and root cause analyses for mechanical and system failures.
  • Design and implement solutions to ensure machines and equipment are installed and functioning according to specifications.
  • Learn and understand existing architectures and make changes as needed for efficiency, safety, and reliability.
  • Conduct onsite visits and coordinate with construction crew and operations personnel
  • Establish and manage relations with independent contractors and vendors.
  • Continuously streamline and optimize existing processes and functions.
  • Prepare material picking lists, purchasing requests, daily and weekly reports to PM.
  • Record progress and update into the construction table.

Required Skills:

  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Strong analysis and problem skills
  • Understanding of Gas Distribution Systems
  • Drawing recognition ability
  • Material identification and calculation
  • Superb attention to detail and self-motivated
  • At least 1-3 years of relevant experience
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office 365
  • Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering or a related field, or equivalent experience

We will be establishing a new company in Dresden, Germany. The company in Germany will be a subsidiary of Taiwan Puritic Corp, founded in 1996. The company serves the constantly innovating semiconductor industry with high-precision equipment, systems, and services. Delivering professional facility turnkey projects and system engineering services to the semiconductor fabrication industry are the company's specialties. We are rapidly growing and expanding and are now looking for talented members to join our team.
