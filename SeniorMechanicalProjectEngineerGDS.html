Senior Mechanical Project Engineer: GDS

Job description

We are looking for several passionate and motivated Senior Mechanical Project Engineers for Gas Distribution Systems (GDS) who will work with our Engineering and Construction teams to support and facilitate the GDS in our large-scale construction project. This candidate will have an interface with the Construction team to support planning and execution at different stages of the construction process. This candidate will also collaborate with the Engineering team to support the creation, modification, and implementation of gas delivery systems and assets. We are looking for individuals who prioritize safety, innovation, cost-effectiveness, and excellence.

This position will be based in Dresden, Germany

Duties:

  • Implement detailed scope and collaboration with facility project engineers.
  • Managing projects from initiation to completion; communicating project updates to other team members, clients, and administrative staff; ensuring that Exponent meets its deadlines and producing exceptional work product; and identifying business development opportunities.
  • Advise CAD drafters in developing the piping design.
  • Supervise all construction types of work including pipe drawing, piping, wall, and floor opening, pipe rack, equipment foundation, equipment locating and welding argon management, management of electric panels for construction, punch management, and improvement.
  • Learn and understand existing architectures and make changes as needed for efficiency, safety, and reliability.
  • Conduct onsite visits and coordinate with construction crew and operations personnel
  • Establish and manage relations with independent contractors and vendors.
  • Continuously streamline and optimize existing processes and functions.
  • Prepare material picking lists, purchasing requests, and daily and weekly reports to the project managers.
  • Record progress and update the construction table.

Required Education and Experience:

  • At least 5-8 years of relevant experience.
  • Bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering or a related field, or equivalent experience.
  • Construction site experience
  • Previous experience in semiconductor fabs

Required Skills:

  • Excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Can understand BIM and CAD drawings or is willing to learn.
  • Mechanical material identification and calculation.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office 365 (Word, Excel,Powerpoint etc).

Preferred Skills:

  • Understanding of gas distribution systems.
